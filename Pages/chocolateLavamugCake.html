Chocolate Lava Mug Cake Recipie:

Makes 1 Serving. 10-15 min cook time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 Cup of Flour
  • 1/4 Cup of Granulated Sugar
  • 2 TBSP Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 TSP Baking Powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 3 TBSP Unsalted Butter (Melted)
  • 3 TBSP Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1oz Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 1 TBSP Water

Procedure:

  1. In a mug, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Stir well with fork.
  3. Add in milk, egg, butter, and vanilla. Stir.
  4. Place chocolate chips in center of mixture.
  5. Drizzle water over batter.
  6. Microwave for 1-2 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes.
