Buttermilk Biscuit Recipie:

Makes 12 Biscuits. 45 min cooktime.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 Stick Unsalted Butter (Cold)
  • 1 Cup Buttermilk

Procedure:

  1. Preheat Oven to 450
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut up butter into small pieces and add into bowl.
  4. Mix until butter is incorporated. Should be a rough crumb texture.
  5. Slowly add in buttermilk.
  6. Mix until dough forms.
  7. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and spread to be a half in thick.
  8. Flour a cup and press into dough.
  9. Transfer cut biscuits onto a baking tray.
  10. Repeat steps 8-9 until all dough is used.
  11. Bake for 15-18 minutes.
  12. Brush with butter.(Optional: Garlic Butter)
