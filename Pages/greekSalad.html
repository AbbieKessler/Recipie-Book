Greek Salad Recipie:

Makes 2 Servings. 15-20 min cook time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Large Cucumber
  • 6 Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 Banana Peppers
  • 1/2 Cup of Chickpeas
  • Crumbled Feta Cheese
  • 1/2 TBSP Minced Garlic
  • 1 TBSP Lemon Juice
  • Skyline Greek Salad Dressing
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Optional: Red Onion

Procedure:s

  1. Wash cucumber and tomatoes. Drain chcikpeas.
  2. Slice cucumber vertically and scoop out seeds. Dice cucumber, tomatoes, and banana peppers (also red onion if desired). Add to bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice and garlic to bowl and mix.
  4. Add in chickpeas and mix.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  6. Add dressing and mix.
  7. Top with desired amount of Feta.
  8. Enjoy!
