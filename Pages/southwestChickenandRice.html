Southwest Chicken and Rice Recipie:

Makes 6 Servings. 25-30 min cook time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups White Rice
  • 1 Can of Tomatoes and Chilies
  • 1 Can of Shredded Chicken Breast
  • 1 Can of Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 Can of Sweet Corn
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt, Pepper, Paprika, Chili Powder
  • Optional:Sour Cream

Procedure:

  1. Cook rice according to instuctions.
  2. In a large pot mix Tomatoes and Chilies, Cream of Chicken Soup, Corn(drain first), and Chicken Breast (drain first)
  3. When done, add hot rice to pot.
  4. Stir until combined.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Keep heated until ready to serve.
  7. Sprinkle on cheddar cheese and sour cream, enjoy.
