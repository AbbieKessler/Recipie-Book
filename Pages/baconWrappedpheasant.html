Bacon Wrapped Pheasant Recipie:

Makes 5 Servings. min cook time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 1 TSP Sugar
  • 10 Pheasant Breasts
  • 1 Package of Bacon (not thick cut)
  • Spice Rub
  • Honey Mustard Sauce

Procedure:

  1. Create Brine by mixing water, salt, and sugar.
  2. Allow Breasts to soak in brine for a minimum of 2 hours. (removes gaminess)
  3. Warm Smoker to 350F
  4. Drain excess brine.
  5. Season both sides of breast.
  6. Wrap each breast with 1-2 piecces of bacon.
  7. Cook on smoker for 10 mins. Flip and cook an additional 6-8 minutes.
  8. Brush both sides of Pheasant with Honey Mustard Sauce with 3 mins left.
  9. Once breast reaches an internal temperature of 145F remove and allow to rest for 10-15 minutes. (ensures pheasant does not overcook)
  10. Serve with extra sauce.
